Recipe Recommender

So how does it work?

Step One Collect the data

Recipe Recommender is backed by a database of recipes collected from the food2fork API. San Francisco restaurant menu data were collected from Yelp.

Step Two Featurize the text

Text from restaurant menu items and descriptions as well as recipe ingredient lists were converted into numeric vectors using Tfidf, a method of numerically representing words in a document according to how often they appear. I used the Python gensim module to make the conversion.

Step Three Calculate similarities

Once the text have been vectorized, I calculate the cosine similiarity to find the nearest neighbors and return those recipes as suggestions.
